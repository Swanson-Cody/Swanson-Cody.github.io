About the Author

My name is Cody Swanson.

I am currently enrolled with BYUI Online. Time For Ukes is being created as part of a semester long project from B 250 - Web Business Creation.

While growing up I always thought it would be neat to learn how to play the ukulele. However, it wasn't until the age of 27 that I actually bought a $20.00 laminate ukulele and began to learn.

Time For Ukes is a growing project of mine. The goal is to make learning the ukulele simple and fun. This site will eventually incorporate videos and pdf documents that will help make learning how to play just that: Simple and fun.
