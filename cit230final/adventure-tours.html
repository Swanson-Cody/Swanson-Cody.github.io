The Mountain Spoke

Want an adventure?

We offer daily, summer, bike adventures with our expert guides leading the way through the wonders of nature around Bozeman, Montana.

  • Bozeman-Beauty Adventure
    • Want to enjoy the bounteous beauty of the great outdoors? This adventure is perfect for immersing yourself with the wonders of nature. Bike on a smooth trail as one of our bike guides narrates what Bozeman, Montana has to offer in regards to vegetation and wildlife.
    • Difficulty: Mild
    • Length: 2 hours
    • Location: Starts at 3950 Valley Commons Drive Bozeman, MT
    • Cost: $60.00 (bike rental included)
    • How to purchase:Call, or visit us to make your purchase. Our address and phone number can be found on our: Contact Us page.
  • Montana-Max Adventure
    • Enjoy pushing yourself to your limits? Do you want to pit yourself against the wilds of Montana? If so, this adventure is for you! Montana-Max features a grueling bike trail, lead by one of our top guides, where you will endure a rewarding challenge of conquering difficult terrain.
    • Difficulty: Hard
    • Length: 5 hours
    • Location: Starts at 3950 Valley Commons Drive Bozeman, MT
    • Cost: $150.00 (bike rental, commemorative t-shirt, and food included)
    • How to purchase:Call, or visit us to make your purchase. Our address and phone number can be found on our: Contact Us page.
