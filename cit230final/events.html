Annual Event: Cycle the Continental Divide!

  • Date: July 6, 2018
  • Route:
    1. Canada - Banff, AB to Rooseville, MT
    2. Rooseville, MT to Polaris, MT
    3. Polaris, MT to South Pass City, WY
    4. South Pass City, WY to Silverthorne, CO
    5. Silverthorne, CO to Platoro, CO
    6. Platoro, CO to Pie Town, NM
    7. Pie Town, NM to Antelope Wells, NM
  • Cost: $20.00 (Will go towards charity. Map of route will be provided. Additional maps may be purchased as desired.)
  • Requirements: Must be 18 years of age or older. Must have your own bike. Must bring your own food, water, and sleeping supplies.
