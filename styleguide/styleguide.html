Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of Time for Ukes is to teach the basics about Ukuleles. This will help empower people to enjoy learning and playing the ukulele. Time for Ukes will teach about the structure of the ukulele and how to use that knowledge to begin playing the ukulele.

Target Audience

The age range of my audience will be geared towards the young and old. That is because my audience is focused on those who aspire to learn the ukulele. This appeals to any gender, age, or ethicity. Time for Ukes is focused upon those who live in english speaking countries as my site will be in english. Time for Ukes will allow a diverse range of people to learn the ukulele. Whether they live in the city or without, since the site is free to use, anyone with access to the site can benefit from the content. Visitors to the site can take as long as they want to learn from the content. Time for Ukes is accessible through all the common devices available, allowing for ease of use.

Color Palette

The colors I chose will help to create a more natural vibe that compliments the look and feel of ukuleles. The Saddlebrown is a nice dark earthy color to give contrast to the Goldenrod and Navajowhite. I also added Burlywood to further compliment the color scheme and add more breadth of color.

Typography

I decided to use Bitter as my heading font because it is practical. It is easily readable from various device sizes. Meanwhile, it has thick, but consistent stroke weight. Catches the eye without being too distracting. Meanwhile, for the body font I decided to use Roboto. This font is part of the sans-serif category. The absent serifs and open curves help make the font easily readable in large portions. The contrast to the thick serifs found in the Bitter font are appealing.
