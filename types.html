Ukulele Types

Soprano

The soprano ukulele is the smallest, traditional class of ukulele available. It is the most common and cheapest available in the marketplace. The music it produces are what is commonly accepted as the sound associated with ukuleles. The space between the strings are very short and may pose a challenge for beginners due to this.

Concert/Alto

The concert ukulele, also known as the alto ukulele, is a popular class of ukulele. It is larger than the soprano but still maintains a lot of the sounds expected from the traditional ukulele.

Tenor

The tenor ukulele is becoming more popular as musicians like to perform with them. This is due to their larger size and deeper range of sound. The space between the strings are much wider compared to a soprano ukulele. Those with larger hands may find the tenor more comfortable to handle.

Baritone

The baritone ukulele provides the deepest tone. This ukulele is the least common class of ukulele. This is due to the fact that it is similar to a guitar in size and sound.
